Istanbul is a city that connects two continents, Europe and Asia, across the Bosphorus Strait. It is known for its rich history that spans from ancient Constantinople to modern Istanbul. The city is home to impressive monuments such as the Blue Mosque, Hagia Sophia Basilica and Topkapi Palace, as well as vibrant markets and unique cuisine that fuses Eastern and Western influences.

London is one of the most important and cosmopolitan cities in the world. As the capital of the United Kingdom, it is a globally renowned cultural, financial and political center. With iconic landmarks such as Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, and the London Eye, London is also renowned for its cultural diversity, West End theaters, world-class museums such as the British Museum and a vibrant fashion and music scene.

Dubai is a futuristic city in the middle of the desert, known for its impressive skyscrapers, luxurious shopping malls and unique tourist attractions such as the Burj Khalifa, the world's tallest building, and the man-made Palm Jumeirah archipelago. It is a center of global commerce and luxury tourism, offering experiences ranging from desert safaris to high-end shopping and vibrant nightlife.

Antalya is a coastal city in southern Turkey, famous for its white sandy beaches and turquoise waters of the Mediterranean Sea. It is a popular tourist destination thanks to its year-round warm climate, its well-preserved Roman ruins such as those of Perge and Aspendos, as well as its old town with cobblestone streets, boutiques and restaurants overlooking the sea.

Paris is the city of love and light, known for its elegance, history and culture. It is home to the iconic Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, the Louvre (the most visited museum in the world) and tree-lined avenues such as the Champs-Elysées. Paris is famous for its refined cuisine, haute couture fashion, stunning art and romantic atmosphere that attracts visitors from all over the world.

Hong Kong es una región administrativa especial de China, conocida por ser un centro financiero global y un puerto internacional. Es una vibrante metrópolis que combina rascacielos modernos con tradiciones chinas antiguas. Hong Kong es famosa por su espectacular horizonte, su vida nocturna animada, su comida callejera deliciosa, mercados bulliciosos como el Ladies' Market y su diversidad cultural única.

Noma (Copenhagen, Denmark)

Founded by chef René Redzepi, Noma has been a world reference in gastronomy for its innovative Nordic cuisine. The restaurant is known for its focus on local and seasonal ingredients, as well as its creative presentation and combination of flavors. Noma has been awarded multiple Michelin stars and has topped the list of the World's 50 Best Restaurants on several occasions.

Geranium (Copenhagen, Denmark)

Also located in Copenhagen, Geranium is led by chef Rasmus Kofoed. This three Michelin-starred restaurant is noted for its focus on modern Danish haute cuisine. Geranium is located on the eighth floor of the Parken soccer stadium, offering panoramic views of the city. Kofoed's dishes are known for their artistic presentation and meticulous use of organic and biodynamic ingredients.

Asador Etxebarri (Atxondo, Spain)

Located in the small town of Atxondo in the Basque Country, Asador Etxebarri is famous for its grilled cuisine. Chef Victor Arguinzoniz uses traditional grilling techniques to enhance the natural flavors of high quality ingredients. With a focus on simplicity and authenticity, dishes range from fresh seafood to meats and vegetables, all prepared to perfection over coals.

Central (Lima, Peru)

Central, run by chefs Virgilio Martínez and Pía León, is known for its exploration of Peruvian ecosystems through gastronomy. Central's tasting menu takes diners on a journey through Peru's diverse altitudes, from the sea to the mountains to the jungle. Local and unconventional ingredients play a crucial role in each dish, reflecting the country's biodiversity and cultural richness.
